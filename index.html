About Tarot & My Approach

Tarot is not about seeing the future — it's about seeing yourself. My readings aim to help you explore patterns, reflect on situations, and better understand your own intuition and choices. Please don’t approach tarot during moments of strong emotional distress or when looking for definitive answers. I don't read on health (including mental health and pregnancy), children, pets, death, deceased people, or missing persons. Thank you for respecting these boundaries and treating tarot as a space for self-discovery rather than prediction.
